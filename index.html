Our Story

Passion on every plate

Founded in 2014 by Chef Marco Bellini, Bella Tavola began as a small neighborhood trattoria with a simple mission: serve honest, delicious food made from the freshest ingredients.

Every dish is prepared from scratch daily. Our pasta is handmade each morning. Our produce comes from local farms within 50 miles. And our bread is baked fresh throughout the day.

We believe great food brings people together. Whether it's a romantic dinner for two or a family celebration, every meal at Bella Tavola is an experience to savor.
